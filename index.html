Suds For Spuds

After many trials and setbacks, A Minor Error is finally releasing it's debut EP "Suds For Spuds". The four track disc will satiate all fans of heavy, progressive music. Combining elements of classic prog and the new school, crushing flair of bands like Between the Buried and Me and Pain of Salvation, "Suds For Spuds" pairs modern prog with a degree of nostalgia that will leave the listener itching for more
