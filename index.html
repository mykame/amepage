About

Formed in 2013, A Minor Error has built a name for themselves in Philly's very small and nearly non-existant Prog Scene. Garnering the support of promoter's and venues of the tri-state area, A.M.E was given the supporting act role nearly a dozen times for National touring acs, from The Contortionist, Chon, and most recently Intevals
